The Best Movies According to Melissa

My top 3 movies of all time.

Harry Potter Series

This is simply one of the best movie renditions of one of my favorite book series. Most movies depicting books are not as good as the book but I thought they did well with these event though plenty was still different or left out of the movie completely.

Easy A

This would likely not be on many people's top 3 favorites, but it is a familiar favorite for me and one that I can just watch if I need to unwind or destress. Plus, Emma Stone is a favorite of actress of mine.

Avatar

Artistically this is one of my all time favorites to watch simply because it is such a beautiful world that was created and this is a movie that really wanted to showcase some awesome graphics. Visually it is stunning.
